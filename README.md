# 📊 Sales Data Analysis using Advanced Excel

## Objective
To analyze regional and product-level sales performance using Advanced Excel, and build an interactive dashboard that highlights revenue trends, profitability, and top-performing sales representatives for business decision-making.

## Dataset
- 1,200+ sales order records
- Fields include Order Date, Region, City, Category, Product, Sales Representative, Quantity, Unit Price, Discount %, Payment Mode, and Total Sales
- Cleaned for missing values, duplicates, and formatting inconsistencies

## Key Skills Demonstrated
- Advanced Excel Formulas: IF, SUMIFS, COUNTIFS, XLOOKUP/VLOOKUP, INDEX-MATCH, TEXT functions
- Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts
- Interactive Dashboard with Slicers
- KPI Calculation & Business Reporting
- Data Cleaning & Structuring

## Analysis Performed
- Regional sales performance comparison
- Category-wise and product-wise revenue breakdown
- Monthly sales trend analysis
- Top-performing sales representative ranking
- Discount impact on overall revenue
- City-wise sales distribution

## Key Business Questions Answered
- Which region generated the highest sales?
- Which product category contributed the most revenue?
- Who are the top-performing sales representatives?
- What are the monthly sales trends?
- Which cities generated the highest sales?
- What is the impact of discounts on revenue?
- Which products have the highest sales volume?

## KPIs Calculated
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Total Orders
- Average Order Value
- Discount Analysis
